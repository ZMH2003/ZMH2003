Honey Muffins

Total time: 30 Min

makes: 1 Dozen

Auther: Grammy Liz

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup honey

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powderand salt. In a small bowl, combine egg, milk, butter and honey. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
  2. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, 15-18 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack. Serve warm
